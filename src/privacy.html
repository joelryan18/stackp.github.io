Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information AXON ("we", "us") collects when you visit this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it short and honest — the same tolerance we hold our product to.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us — if you request access, we store the email address you submit so we can respond.
  • Usage data — standard server logs and analytics: pages viewed, approximate location (country/city level), device and browser type, and referring pages.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — small files stored by your browser, described below.

2. Cookies

We use essential cookies to remember preferences (for example, your cookie-consent choice) and analytics cookies to understand how the site is used so we can improve it. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies in the consent banner, and you can delete or block cookies at any time in your browser settings.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this and/or other sites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of a range of third-party vendors' cookies at www.aboutads.info.

4. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve this website.
  • To respond to access requests and inquiries you send us.
  • To measure traffic and performance in aggregate.
  • To display advertising, where you have consented to it.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run the site (hosting, analytics, advertising as described above), or when required by law.

6. Data retention & security

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete it. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with access controls.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal data we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@stackwith.me — we respond within 30 days.

8. Children

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be flagged on the homepage.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy: privacy@stackwith.me, or see our contact page.
