[ SIG.03 · CORE ]

Three instruments in the core.

01 / PLANNER

A planner that thinks in outcomes.

AXON decomposes a goal into a tree of steps, simulates the likely result of each, and commits to the path with the highest expected success — then re-plans the moment reality disagrees.

02 / ROUTER

A router that never stalls.

Work is dispatched across a swarm at the edge. Failures retry, slow paths reroute, and hand-offs between agents happen in under forty milliseconds — no human waiting on a queue.

03 / LEDGER

A ledger you can trust.

Every decision, tool call and side effect is written to an immutable trace. Scope what an agent may touch, gate the risky moves behind approval, and audit any run down to the token.
