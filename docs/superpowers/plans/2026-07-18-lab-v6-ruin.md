## The fiction

The descent has always implied the shaft is natural. It isn't. Each chapter
holds one hand-built remnant of whoever cut it: a broken hexagonal Gate the
camera passes through at the surface; a spiral of sheared strata slabs in
the descent; a machined three-arm Cradle holding the core heart; and — the
bookend — the *intact* twin of the Gate far overhead at resurface. Your
first full discharge seats a gem fragment in the Cradle's keystone socket,
permanently. The survey remembers.
